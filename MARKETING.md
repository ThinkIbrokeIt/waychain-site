## Technical FAQ (For Articles/Blogs)

**Q: SHA256 or Keccak256 for function selectors?**  
A: SHA256 is native. 0x21 precompile provides Keccak256 for EVM compatibility.

**Q: Why SHA256 instead of Keccak256?**  
A: Bitcoin compatibility, better tooling maturity, auditable consensus.

**Q: Can I use Solidity?**  
A: Yes, via 0x21 precompile for EVM-compatible hashing. Native contracts use SHA256.

**Q: How do I become a validator?**
A: Get Dox_Dev Level 2 via /badge. 200 slots available.
