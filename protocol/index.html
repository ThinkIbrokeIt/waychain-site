Privacy (ZK)

Cross-Chain Attestation

0x0000…001F
Witnesses events on external chains (witnessEvent/getAttestation/challengeAttestation). Selector-based. Requires relayed chain proofs — advanced; not callable from this read-only UI.

Account Recovery

0x0000…0011
