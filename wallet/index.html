Getting Started

For New Users

  1. Generate a new wallet: Click the "Create New Wallet" button below. This generates a new cryptographic key pair directly in your browser.
  2. Save your private key: Your private key is shown only once. Copy it and store it securely offline (paper, hardware wallet, encrypted USB). Never share this with anyone.
  3. Your address: Your WayChain address (starting with 0x) is your public identifier for receiving funds.
  4. Fund your wallet: Use the faucet button to get test WAY, or receive from another wallet.

Important

WayChain uses Ed25519 keys (not seed phrases). Your security depends entirely on protecting your private key. If lost, funds are unrecoverable.
