Step 1: Get Your Wallet

A blockchain wallet is like a bank account, but you hold the keys. WayChain uses Ed25519 signatures — different from Bitcoin/Ethereum.

Generate Keys (Command Line)

$ curl -X POST https://api.waychain.org \ -H "Content-Type: application/json" \ -d '{"jsonrpc":"2.0","method":"way_generateWallet","params":[],"id":1}'

Or use the web wallet at /wallet:

  • Your seed phrase (12 words) — write this down. Never share it.
  • Your public address (0x...) — this is like your account number.
  • Your private key — never share this either. Keep it secret.

⚠️ Save your seed phrase

If you lose it, you lose your tokens. There's no "forgot password" on the blockchain. No customer support. You are your own bank.

Step 2: Get Test Tokens

Two ways to get WAY tokens:

Option A: CashApp Bitcoin Bridge (Real Route)

  1. Open CashApp → Send Bitcoin
  2. Send $1 BTC to the WayChain treasury address
  3. Your TXID proves the send (6+ confirmations)
  4. Receive ~100 WAY deposited to your WayChain address

This is the bridge. Treasury holds the BTC. Protocol credits your address.

Option B: Request from Faucet

Go to /faucet. Paste your address. Click "Request 10 WAY". Get tokens in 1 second.

  1. Go to Faucet
  2. Paste your WayChain address (starts with 0x)
  3. Click "Request 10 WAY"
  4. Check your balance in 1 second (links to explorer)

Option C: Send Bitcoin, Receive 1WAY

Send real BTC from any wallet and receive 1WAY — the Bitcoin-backed stablecoin — directly to your WayChain address.

  1. Send BTC to the 1WAY bridge address (shown on /bridge)
  2. Wait for confirmations (6+ blocks, ~1 hour)
  3. 1WAY credits automatically at 1:1 peg to your WayChain address
  4. Use 1WAY anywhere on WayChain — swap, send, hold in treasury

1WAY = Bitcoin on WayChain. Fully backed. Send BTC, get 1WAY. Send 1WAY, get BTC. The bridge is the closed loop.

Balance check: curl -X POST https://api.waychain.org \ -d '{"jsonrpc":"2.0","method":"eth_getBalance","params":["0xYourAddress","latest"],"id":1}'

Step 3: Send Your First Transaction

Transfer tokens to another address. Watch it get confirmed in 1 second.

$ curl -X POST https://api.waychain.org \ -H "Content-Type: application/json" \ -d '{ "jsonrpc":"2.0", "method":"eth_sendTransaction", "params":[{ "from":"0xYourAddress", "to":"0xRecipientAddress", "value":"0x1000" }], "id":1 }'

What happened:

  • Your transaction entered the pool
  • A validator picked it up within 1 second
  • The block was finalized
  • The recipient got the tokens

What Is a Blockchain?

A blockchain is a digital ledger that everyone can read but no one can secretly edit. Instead of one company holding all records, copies exist on thousands of computers worldwide.

Key Differences from Standard EVM

WayChain uses SHA256, not keccak256

All function selectors, storage keys, and transaction hashes use SHA256. Standard EVM tooling uses keccak256 — adapt your ABI selectors.
