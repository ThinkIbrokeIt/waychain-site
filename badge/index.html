Get Verified

L1 — Social verification
Link your GitHub, X (Twitter), or other identity to your WayChain address via a signed message.
Coming soon: self-serve verification portal.


L2 — Developer badge
Complete L1 verification and submit a developer profile. Curators review and approve.
Required to deploy contracts on WayChain.


L3 — Curator badge
Invite-only. L3 curators are the most trusted members of the WayChain community.
They approve L2 applications and help maintain the network.
