One Human, One Vote

Every Dox_Dev-verified human runs one validator with equal voting power. No whales. No vote buying. 200 badges = 200 validators.

Self-Sovereign

Bitcoin-backed stablecoin (1WAY). Native oracle attestations. Dead Man's Switch inheritance protocol. No wrapping, no bridging.

Anti-Extractive

Fixed gas fees, state rent with 60% burn, sqrt-weighted proposer lottery, no MEV. The chain doesn't extract from its users.

Bitcoin → 1WAY

Native BTC deposits to Treasury mint 1WAY stablecoin. No wrapping. No bridging. 1WAY = $1.00 (troy oz silver equivalent).

Gold Claims

Professional geologists, surveyors, engineers submit verified claims. Oracle Aggregator (0x0C) accepts submissions. MRT Registry (0x20) tracks commodity rights.

Community Work

Build, verify, contribute. Earn WAY tokens. Dox_Dev Level 1 (Lantern) through Level 3 (Lighthouse). No whale capture.

DEX Swap

WAY to 1WAY conversion with no MEV. Provide liquidity. Earn fees. Fixed gas fees. Anti-fragile economics.
